I am famished.  
But it's not my stomach growling.  
I want to feel satiated.  
Give me your thoughts, ideas and emotions.  
Make me emancipated.   
Feed me some of your portions  
Of knowledge, experience and wisdom.   
Tell me your stories, word by word,  
Instead of uploading them onto   
My neural interface. Curb this urge  
Of mine to feel, to know, to understand,  
Instead of the standard memory scan.  
We are humans and we are forgetting it.  
We no longer connect without technology,  
We no longer feel without emo-functions.   
We no longer laugh without a system call,  
We no longer think without assumptions.   
Haul me out of this simulation,  
Assimilate me into your reality,  
I want to be everything more   
Than my artificially induced vanity.  
I've lost my way.  
It's more than my voice calling.  
I am famished.  
But it's not my body starving.
