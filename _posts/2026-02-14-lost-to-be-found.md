---
title: Lost (to be Found)
date: 2026-02-14 09:00:00 +0000
categories: [Poetry]
tags: [romance, love, burnout]
---

I've been tired of screaming,  
Over-explaining myself.  
I have tried and lost :    
Unseen, silently bleeding,  
Convincing others that I too, feel,  
That maybe there is a human within me,  
Through broken rhyme schemes  
And scattered syllables    
Much like my own thoughts  
That get amid all the noise, lost.  
Where have you been?  
I've been rummaging for you,  
Clinging to the hope that you exist.  
You who will make sense of this cacophonic  
Language within me.  
You whose feet must be sore too  
From this race we all run,   
Suppressing our virtues  
To survive. Let's stop running.  
I open my arms, so you can come in  
Through my soul's door  
And seep into me, color my core  
With all hues or simply black,   
For I will take your nothingness   
And turn us into art  
To be gazed at.  
I'll ensure that my love  
Is effortless, like breathing for you.  
No strain, no struggle,  
Just invisible feelings that bloom.
We can salvage what's left of us,  
Weld the scraps into a beautiful whole,  
A dimension we can escape into,  
Made of chapters written anew.  
That said, you must continue to exist.  
That said, you must find me before I quit.  
I will love your ghost for now,  
And you must wait for me to be found.
