---
title: The Convict
date: 2025-11-14 09:00:00 +0000
categories: [Poetry]
tags: [love, letting go, regret, bitterness, recollection]
---

I'm evil in the eyes of you
Where once I lived until
I shattered and withered away
With every hope I failed.
You knew all the oddities of me,
The sculpt of my body,
How my heart would beat
In your company, calmly.
I kept you within me,
Carried you with my bags.
I was alone but never lonely-
You misunderstood this fact.
I saw your love morph
From longing, to rage,
To the hatred it is now
That we saw us break.
I couldn't give back enough.
I couldn't fucking be enough.
Your usual world and the usual needs
Weighed heavier and heavier on me.
Your kind efforts became a debt,
Your loneliness a responsibility,
Your tears my sin,
All tearing through my skin.
You will not comprehend
My reason to leave.
I spared both our lives, 
Amputated us and
Cauterized the bleed.
For the sickness that remained of us
Was no longer love, not even friendship.
Just two tired humans extending the fight,
Neither having the guts to end it.
Your mere existence meant hope to me,
While I struggled to make you believe
That I too, had loved,
Just not how you wanted to see.

The cold winter approaches now,
Venom circulates within.
The demons chase my soul, derelict
I keep running, I'm the convict.
