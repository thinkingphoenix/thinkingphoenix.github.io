---
title: The Horror of Beauty 
date: 2026-04-02 09:00:00 +0000
categories: [Poetry]
tags: [beauty, rage, disgust, discrimination, delusion]
---



Fuck off, you pretty humans  
With glowing skin and a soul corrupt.  
You entitled imbeciles who consider  
A favor what is basic human conduct.  
Make-up or muscles won't conceal  
How unsightly you are underneath.  
When your mask fucking falls off,  
I'll see how you lie about how you feel.  
You are the reason I see  
Rot much before beauty.  
That I sense judgement  
Much before empathy.  
Such is how I feel at times.  
At times when it hurts to be kind.  
To hold you to your word  
While you sort me by my worth.  
With a sense of worth you've built by filth.  
With your twisted, flawed, fucked-up mind  
That sees me not as a person but a checklist of criteria fulfilled.  
To wish ill for you would be  
A waste of my thoughts,  
But I can at least yell what's in my head,  
While you can only sleep with what's in your    bed.  
I will forget how you treated me  
Because you've nothing except a body:    
You're just a photogenic corpse.  
But I will mold my words into fists  
So that I can punch through your vanity   
And watch how your charm morphs  
Into the abomination you really are.  
You, who are terrified of scars.
You, who can no longer hurt me.  
You, whom I will show no courtesy.
