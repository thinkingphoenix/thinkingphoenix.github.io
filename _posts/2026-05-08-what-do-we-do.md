---
title: What Do We Do?
date: 2026-05-08 09:00:00 +0000
categories: [Poetry]
tags: [hope, despair, togetherness, self-exploration]
---

What do we do
When the night falls and the dark enshrouds us?  
When all life succumbs to the silence around us?  

If possible, light a lamp. Or just flick a match.  

If possible, lead the way. Or just hold onto a hand.  


What do we do  

When the angst within turns the heartbeats irregular?  

When all that keeps us awake turns gargantuan from granular?  

If possible, put it to words. Or just let your eyes speak. 

If possible, hear someone out. Or just admit when you feel weak.   


What do we do  

When there's nowhere left for us to belong to?  

When they're too apathetic to see what we've gone through?  

If possible, build a refuge with twigs. Or just chase the silhouette of hope.  

If possible, lend your own shoulder. Or just reach out for help when you choke.  


What do we do  

When we turn invisible despite the cloak of flesh we wear?  

When the grand total of our emotions is just despair?  

If possible, paint colors on blank faces. Or just borrow a hue from the skies.  

If possible, reconsider giving up. Or just survive until reinforcements arrive.  


What do we do  

If none of it exists and we're falling apart?  


...be your own guardian angel.  

Keep sewing back your wings,  

Evolve to see in the dark   

Until the moment of your rising.  

You are the strong but  

The uncertainty principle exists.  

At an instance you could be weak  

If measured while your momentum builds.
