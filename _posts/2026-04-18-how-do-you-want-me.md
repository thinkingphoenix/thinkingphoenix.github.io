---
title: How do you want me?   
date: 2026-04-18 09:00:00 +0000
categories: [Poetry]
tags: [lust, erotica, passion, sex, depravity]
---

Chained?  
Or holding your reins?  
Pristine, plain   
Or blood-stained?  
Partly sane   
Or fully deranged?  
Splaying my brain   
To show your name   
In cursive, ingrained?  
My tongue  
In a frenzy, passionate  
For your taste,  
Set ablaze  
By your lusty gaze,  
As it paints  
The secret lanes  
Of your womanhood lain  
Bare-breasted, like open terrain,  
In this divine/depraved game
We play
As we remain 
To our desires, enslaved.
