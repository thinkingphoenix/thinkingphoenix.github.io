---
title: Emotional Floodgates
date: 2026-06-05 09:00:00 +0000
categories: [Poetry]
tags: [emotions, catharsis, journal, outburst]
---
Their mention brings you a smile?  
Tighten your lips. If you smiled,  
Limit how wide it stretches.
Leave no room for any guesses.  

Does their appearance make you  
Want to grab their soul and kiss it?  
Scientifically, you can't. So show restraint.  
Clench your heart and appear sane.   

Are you nervous inside and forget  
Everything in their proximity?  
It happens. Let silence fall.  
Or scavenge topics for small talk.  

Have they turned into your muse?  
Making their way into your very thoughts?  
Well, that's worth serious concern.  
Secretly weave rhymes they can't discern.  

Do you crave to hold their hand  
And trade their suffering for your affection?  
But that would leave you hollower inside.  
Because you can't find yourself in their eyes (yet?).  

Hankering to be in their company  
But doubtful of what they see you as?  
Keep assessing their words, their reactions  
And dissect their expressions into fractions.  

Or rather relinquish what you hold,  
Because you feel exasperated.  
Too hurt, so you dismiss it as hormones.  
Infinite within, yet made of flesh and bones.  

Because you're not entertainment.  
Because you're not a chamber to vent.  
Because you're not a savior.  
You can't carry weights than you heavier.  

But your catharsis exists here.  
In pen, papers, keyboards and texts.  
Eyes locked on to the ceiling,  
Searching for what to write next.  
You can safely write how you feel,  
Instead of imposing it on others.  
You can acknowledge when you're weak,  
And abandon the pursuit of love or its leftovers.
